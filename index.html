Tribute to Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Introduction

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was a visionary scientist, statesman, and teacher. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

  • Defense Research and Development Organization (DRDO)

"If you want to shine like a sun, first burn like a sun."
